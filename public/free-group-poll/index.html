Why one app beats juggling several

  • One place to learn. Your group gets used to a single link and flow instead of a different site for every kind of decision.
  • Made for phones. Every poll type — including the availability grid — is built to work with a thumb, not just a mouse.
  • Timezone-aware. Times show in each person's local zone, so remote and international groups don't have to convert anything.
  • Free, and open to everyone. Share one link and anyone can respond — no cost and no account required to vote.

Frequently asked questions

Is Huddlr really free?

Yes. Creating polls and collecting responses is free, with no paid tier required to run a normal group poll.

What kinds of polls can I run?

Four: a meeting-time vote, an availability grid, a ranked-choice vote on any options, and a quick RSVP or headcount — all from the same app.

Do people need an account?

Voters never need an account — anyone with the link can respond. To create and manage polls you sign in with just your email (a magic link, no password and no credit card).

Does it work on my phone?

Yes. Every poll type is designed to work on any phone, tablet, or laptop straight from the browser — nothing to install.
